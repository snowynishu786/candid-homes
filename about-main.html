About Candid Homes

Welcome to Candid Homes

We provide the best experience for home renovation and design.

Candid Homes started with a home.

When people redesign their home, they start the way these projects often do: with a tall stack of magazines and referrals for home professionals from people they knew. But when those piles of torn out pages failed to make their dream a reality, they often feel stuck. There had to be a better way.

So we built Candid Homes.

A place to browse and save beautiful photos of inspiring homes. A place to find the right design. A place to connect with others who have been there too.

Whether you are decorating a small room or building a custom home, Candid Homes is the best way to find design inspiration and discover products to complete your projects.
